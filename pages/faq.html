Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell products online?

No, we do not offer online shopping. Please visit our store to explore and purchase mobiles and laptops.

Do you provide EMI options?

Yes, we provide Easy EMI options in-store on select bank cards. Please visit our shop to check eligibility and available plans.

Do your products come with a warranty?

Yes, all our mobiles and laptops are brand-new and come with official manufacturer warranty.

Can I check the product before buying?

Absolutely! We encourage our customers to see, compare, and experience the product in-store before making a decision.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash, UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, and EMI (in-store only).

Do you provide repair or servicing?

No, we do not run a repair/service center. However, we guide you to the official brand service center for warranty claims.

What are your working hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday: 10 AM – 9 PM. (Sundays closed).
